LeoAI: AI-Driven Customer Support for Small Businesses

LeoAI is an AI-powered chatbot platform designed to automate customer support for small businesses, with a focus on simplicity, transparency, and faith-driven values. Built for the steadfastcode.tech website, LeoAI delivers a mobile-first, user-friendly experience that helps business owners save time by handling routine customer inquiries efficiently. The chatbot, named Leo, integrates seamlessly into a business’s website, offering a conversational interface that feels personal and trustworthy.

How LeoAI Works: LeoAI’s core feature is its intelligent chatbot, Leo, which uses natural language processing (NLP) to answer up to 80% of common customer questions, such as business hours, services, or order status. Leo learns about a business by scraping its website for key details like hours, contact information, and FAQs, with an option for manual input if no website exists or for additional information not available on their site. A guided setup walkthrough helps owners configure Leo by entering their website URL, selecting learning preferences, and adding custom FAQs. Leo also supports web push notifications to alert owners when a query can’t be answered, ensuring no customer is ignored. Optional integrations, like Messenger, enhance communication, while monthly reports highlight time saved through automation. Customization options allow businesses to tailor Leo’s responses and appearance, fostering a unique customer experience. Leo's backend will efficiently store and structure business information so that Leo can effectively learn about new businesses quickly, and allow the NLP connection (Google Cloud's DialogFlow to start, possibly changing to Hugging Face later if needed) to accurately and quickly provide responses to customer queries. Leo will also offer add-on capabilities for SBO's with differing needs. Businesses with changing product inventory or events can pay extra for LeoRefresh, to allow daily site scraping to keep Leo up to date with any changes. Future features may also include the ability to order directly with Leo for restaurants and cafes, or add items directly to the cart for online stores, and the ability to checkout directly with Leo as well.

General Goal: The goal of LeoAI is to empower small businesses with affordable, cutting-edge AI tools that streamline customer support, freeing owners to focus on their core operations. By automating repetitive tasks, LeoAI aims to save time, boost efficiency, and build stronger customer relationships. The platform strives to be accessible, requiring minimal technical expertise, so any small business can adopt it. LeoAI also seeks to foster trust through ethical AI use, ensuring transparency (e.g., clearly identifying Leo as a chatbot) and aligning with the values of its users.

Target Userbase: LeoAI is tailored for small business owners in Lititz, Pennsylvania, and similar close-knit communities. The primary users are local entrepreneurs—such as café owners, boutique retailers, bakers, or service providers—who lack the resources for dedicated customer support staff but want to maintain excellent service. These businesses typically rely on personal connections with customers and value tools that enhance, rather than replace, their human touch. LeoAI is ideal for owners who are busy, tech-curious but not tech-savvy, and seeking cost-effective solutions to manage customer inquiries.

Philosophy Behind LeoAI: LeoAI is rooted in a faith-driven commitment to serve others with integrity, inspired by Colossians 3:23 (LSB): “Whatever you do, do your work heartily, as for the Lord rather than for men.” This philosophy shapes every aspect of the platform, from its transparent AI interactions to its focus on empowering small businesses to thrive. I created LeoAI to honor God through excellence in work, providing a tool that reflects honesty, reliability, and care for the community. By supporting Lititz’s small businesses, LeoAI aims to strengthen local economies and relationships, ensuring technology serves as a force for good. The platform is designed to be ethical, avoiding manipulative practices and prioritizing clear communication, so users trust Leo as a partner in their success.

Intent Management and Dynamic Responses: LeoAI’s admin dashboard includes a specialized tool for batch intent creation, allowing the platform administrator to efficiently upload or paste JSON files containing multiple Dialogflow intents with their training phrases and responses, streamlining the initial setup of the chatbot’s capabilities. For dynamic responses tailored to each small business, LeoAI employs a backend service that integrates with Dialogflow to process user queries, fetch relevant data from scraped websites (using Cheerio.js) and SBO-provided information, and generate personalized responses in real-time. This dual approach ensures Leo can handle common inquiries (e.g., business hours, services) with minimal manual configuration while adapting to each business’s unique data, enhancing automation and customer engagement without requiring extensive intent management by SBOs.
